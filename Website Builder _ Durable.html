About us

Vitalise.AI is a Mumbai-based company that specializes in comprehending complex medical reports, such as X-rays, MRIs, and blood tests. We provide advice and recommendations based on these reports, including lifestyle changes and consultations with doctors.

In addition, we offer a convenient platform for users to upload their medication prescriptions and receive information about potential side effects and alternative options available in the market. Our goal is to empower individuals to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.
